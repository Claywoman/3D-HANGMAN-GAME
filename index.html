How to Play Hangman

×

1. Game Objective

Guess the hidden word before the Hangman figure is fully drawn! You have a limited number of incorrect guesses (mistakes).

2. Getting Started

  • Choose Difficulty: Select Easy, Medium, or Hard. This determines the length and complexity of the words.
  • Choose Category: Pick a topic like Animals, Countries, or Food. The hidden word will be from this category.
  • Start Game: Click the "START GAME" button to begin!

3. Making Guesses

  • A series of dashes will represent the hidden word.
  • Use the on-screen keyboard to click letters you want to guess.
  • If your guessed letter is in the word, it will appear in all correct positions.
  • If your guessed letter is NOT in the word, a part of the Hangman figure will be drawn, and your "Guesses Left" will decrease.
  • You cannot guess the same letter twice.

4. Winning & Losing

  • Win: If you correctly guess all the letters in the word before the Hangman is complete, you win!
  • Lose: If the Hangman figure is fully drawn before you guess the word, you lose!

5. Sound Effects

The game includes various sound effects for clicks, wins, and losses to enhance your experience. Ensure your volume is adjusted as desired.
